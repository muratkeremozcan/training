Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pound of sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter

Directions

  1. Mix eggs, sugar and butter in a large bowl
  2. Spread into a non-stick dish
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour
  4. Let sit at room temp for 20 minutes
  5. EAT!
